Meet Philip

Philip's Cuts is a multicultural barber shop in Russellville, AR. I've been in business for over 10 years and I've had the pleasure of trimming and shaping the heads of hundreds, if not thousands of wonderful people. I take pride in my work, and people seem to notice - maybe I'm doing something right.

That said, I owe all my success to the community and God above. Here's to another decade of great service and fair prices.

See you soon,
Philip
